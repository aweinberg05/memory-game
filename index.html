Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmaism, Shinkei-suijake, Pexeso, or Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
